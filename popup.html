Before/After Search

Select "Start" to jump to the middle of the video. If what you're looking for is before the middle, click "Before This". If not, click "After This". Repeat this process, clicking "Before" or "After" until you find what you want. Click "Stop" to end the search. Note: Clicking the buttons enough will end the search by itself if it can't find any more points to jump to.
